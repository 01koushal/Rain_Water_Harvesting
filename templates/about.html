About Our Mission

Empowering communities with the data and tools for a sustainable water future.

How It Works

This application provides powerful tools to estimate your rainwater harvesting potential. By combining your specific location details with comprehensive environmental data, we deliver a personalized report to guide your water conservation efforts. Whether you enter your rooftop details manually or draw your property on our interactive map, our goal is to give you an accurate and actionable assessment.

Our Data Sources

Accuracy is at the core of our calculations. We leverage reliable, publicly available datasets for India to ensure our estimates are as precise as possible:

  • Rainfall Data: We utilize district-wise annual rainfall normals from government sources (`district wise rainfall normal.csv`) to determine the average precipitation in your specific area.
  • Groundwater Data: Information on aquifer types and typical groundwater depth is sourced from hydrogeological surveys (`groundwater_data.csv`), helping to assess the feasibility of artificial recharge.

Why Rainwater Harvesting?

Conserve Water

Reduce your reliance on municipal or groundwater sources, saving a precious natural resource.

Recharge Groundwater

Directly replenish local aquifers, combating declining water tables and ensuring long-term water security.

Save Money

Lower your water bills over time by using natural, free rainwater for non-potable needs like gardening and cleaning.
